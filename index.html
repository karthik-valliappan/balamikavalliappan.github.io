About Me

I started learning Bharatanatyam when I was about age 7, from Kalaimamani Dr. M S Sarala at Kalaikoil Dance Academy which follows the style of Valuvur Ramayapillai. With great determination and dedication, I presented my Arangetram under the guidance of my Guru at the age of 11, and I have not looked back ever since. I have made it my ambition to train and nurture passionate students of this art form to find their feet, and enable them to achieve greater heights. I consistently continue to pursue my dream of becoming an internationally recognized Bharatanatyam performer and teacher.

Teaching Experience

Having nearly 8 years of teaching experience at Kailkoil Dance Academy -Katai kudi, and involved in training its students for solo and group dance performances. And started a branch of Kalaikoil Dance Academy at Kilasivalpatti in 2016 that works to promote the Bharatanatyam art and enable the common people to understand and appreci ate it. The school currently has 15 performing students. And as a teacher I presented my first Arangetram of my student on June 2019 at Kilasivalpatti. I have also Choreographed dances for variety of students as a part time and full time dance teacher with a view to classical, folk and other forms of dance.
